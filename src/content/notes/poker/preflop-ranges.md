# Preflop Ranges

Understanding basic preflop gives you the most edge on a table. Identifying fish in a game usually involves seeing what their preflop raising range and VPIP is and if it deviates from the standard. 

6 handed play. Play a little tighter 9 handed.


#### First to raise (RFI)

<div style="margin-left: 1rem;">
<details>
<summary><strong>UTG RFI</strong></summary>

<div style="margin-left: 1rem;">

- 22+, ATo+, A2s+, K8s+, (Q-T)9s+, KJo+, QJo+
- Any suited ace, broadways hands (A-T), pocket pair raise
- Include Broadway-9 suited
- Suited connectors raise at 50% frequency

</div>

</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>HJ RFI</strong></summary>

<div style="margin-left: 1rem;">

- 22+, ATo+, A2s+, K7s+, Q9s+, J9s+, T8s+, 98s, 87s, 76s, 65s, 54s, KJo+, QJo+
- Still no offsuit non broadway hands
- Raising more suited connectors and gappers at 100% frequency

</div>

</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>CO RFI</strong></summary>

<div style="margin-left: 1rem;">

- 22+, A8o+, A2s+, K3s+, KTo+, Q6s+, J7s+, T7s+, 97s, 86s+, 98s, 86s, 76s, 65s, 54s
- Expanding range significantly

</div>

</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>BTN RFI</strong></summary>

<div style="margin-left: 1rem;">

- 22+, A4o+, K8o+, A2s+, K2s+, K9o+, Q2s+, J4s+, T6s+, 96s+, 85s+, 75s+, 64s+, 54s
- Any suited K or Q (arguably J) raises
- Finally offsuit broadways play (barely)

</div>

</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>SB RFI</strong></summary>

<div style="margin-left: 1rem;">

- 22+, A4o+, K8o+, Q9o+, J8o+, T8o+, 98o, A2s+, K2s+, Q2s+, J4s+, T6s+, 96s+, 85s+, 75s+, 64s+, 54s
- BvB widest range!

</div>

</details>
</div>

#### When to 3! vs RFI

<div style="margin-left: 1rem;">

<details>
<summary><strong>vs UTG</strong></summary>

<div style="margin-left: 1rem;">

Note: in a 9 handed game, UTG would be around UTG2~LJ
- as the HJ : 99+, A9s+, A5s–A4s, KTs+, QJs+, AQo+
    - suited connectors (45, 56, 67) very infrequent 3!
- as the CO : same as HJ
    - suited connectors (45, 56, 67) very infrequent 3!
    - add in KQo, QTs, 88 occasionally 
- as the BTN : 88+, A9s+, A5s–A4s, KTs+, QJs+, AQo+
    - Every single suited broadway or offsuit Q and higher
    - suited connectors (56, 67) very infrequent 3!
- as the SB : 99+, ATs+, A5s, KTs+, QTs+, AKo
    - rather tight
    - AQo occasionally

</div>
